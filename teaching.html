Teaching

 

I teach systems courses both at the undergraduate and graduate levels. These include Computer Systems (CSCI 2400) and Design of Operating Systems (CSCI 3753) at the undergraduate level and Distributed Systems (CSCI 5673), Computer Networks (CSCI 5273) and Advanced Operating Systems (CSCI 5573) at the graduate level. In addition, I have taught several special topics courses including Strengthening Democracy through Technology, Sustainable Computing and Dependable Distributed Systems.
