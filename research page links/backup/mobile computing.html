Context Aware Pervasive Mobile Computing 

We have been conducting ground-breaking research in building context aware, pervasive mobile systems. The goal is to develop system develop support to fuse together Internet of Things, social networks, sensor networks, mobility, power management, security, privacy and context awareness to enable highly personalized services to the end users. We have worked on a number of projects within this theme and have developed some exciting, cutting edge systems and services. These include OutWithFriendz to understand mobile group behavior, Panorama to support context monitoring on co-located mobile devices, Data Partitioning to preserve privacy in mobile applications, CID3 to provide support for loosely-coupled personal computing environments, an augmented reality mobile application to facilitate healthy grocery shopping and optimizing power consumption in multicore smartphones.

I have helped establish The NSF IUCRC  (Industry University Cooperative Research Center) on Pervasive Personalized  Intelligence and am serving as a co-site director of this center.
