Welcome!

Welcome to my website!

I am a professor and associate chair in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Colorado, Boulder.

My research, broadly defined, is in the areas of computer systems and social computing. Topics include distributed computing, mobile and pervasive computing, cybersafety, dependable systems, wireless sensor networks, social computing as well as security and privacy.

In addition to my research, I teach both undergraduate and graduate level courses in a variety of areas such as computer systems, advanced operating systems and distributed systems. When I am not teaching or doing research I love to spend time with my family hiking and exploring the beautiful Rocky Mountains!
